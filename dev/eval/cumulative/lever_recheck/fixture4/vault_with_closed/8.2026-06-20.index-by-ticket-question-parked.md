We tried re-indexing Deskmate's KB articles keyed by the phrasing of the support ticket whose question originally prompted each article's creation, instead of the article's own title/body content. Precision@3 rose to ~0.95 on the narrow subset of tickets that echoed that exact originating phrasing, but fell well below baseline for the majority of tickets phrased differently — dropping overall precision@3 further. Parked as a net quality regression outside that narrow subset.
