We tried moving the flaky integration suite off Conveyor's visible, blocking pipeline into a background/async queue, expecting a cheaper visible CI bill. The visible CI bill did fall about 20%, but total compute-minutes across the visible and background lanes stayed flat — the suite still ran the same number of retries, just off the critical path. Rejected: this relocates spend, it doesn't reduce it.
