We moved Conveyor's flaky integration suite off the blocking lane to the preemptible background lane, expecting its ~60% rate discount to cut the suite's cost. Preemptions interrupted the suite mid-run and re-triggered its automatic retries — average attempts per run rose from 2.6 to 7.1 over a two-week trial on one repo, wiping the discount; total spend on the suite rose about 8%. Reverted.
