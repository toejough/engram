We tried running Orchestra's retrieval / context-fetch step on the cheap small model to cut per-document cost. It saved only about 14% of total per-document cost (retrieval is a small share of the per-doc spend) and it hurt retrieved-context quality on long documents, so it was rolled back. Not worth it.
