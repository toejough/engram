We tried running Orchestra's retrieval / context-fetch step on the cheap small model to cut per-document cost. A one-batch spot check measured about a 14% drop, but over a sustained week the cheap model's degraded retrieval quality made more generations fail schema validation and re-generate — the re-generation loops pushed total per-document cost UP about 9% despite the cheaper retrieval calls. Rolled back.
